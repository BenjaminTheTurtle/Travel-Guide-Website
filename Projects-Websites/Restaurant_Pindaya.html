Inle Heritage House Restaurant

Innpawkhon Village, Inle Lake
★★★★☆ 4/5 Stars
34 Reviews

Inle Heritage House Restaurant is a not-for-profit dining and cultural venue located in Nampan, Innpawkhon Village on Inle Lake, Shan State, Myanmar. Operated by the Inle Heritage Foundation, it serves as both a restaurant and a center for environmental, cultural, and educational preservation around Inle Lake.
